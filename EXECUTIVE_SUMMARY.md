## Methodology

### Data Source

**Primary:** World Bank World Development Indicators
- Indicator: GC.TAX.TOTL.GD.ZS
- Definition: Tax revenue as % of GDP
- Coverage: 1972-2024
- Quality: Internationally standardized

### Analysis Methods

**Statistical Analysis:**
- Summary statistics (mean, median, std dev, min, max)
- Trend analysis (linear regression for direction)
- Year-over-year changes
- Decade-by-decade evolution
- Global comparisons

**Quality Tiers:**
- **Tier 1:** OECD + major economies, 30+ years avg
- **Tier 2:** Emerging markets, 20-30 years avg
- **Tier 3:** Developing countries, variable coverage

### Limitations

**Data Coverage:**
- Central government tax revenue only
- Subnational taxes may not be fully captured
- Some countries have gaps in time series
- Latest data may lag 1-2 years

**Measurement:**
- Tax definitions vary slightly across countries
- GDP measurement affects ratios
- Informal economy not captured
- Does not include non-tax revenue

**Analysis:**
- Correlations not causation
- Country-specific context critical
- Historical comparisons complicated by changing definitions
